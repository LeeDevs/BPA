Chapter Guidelines and Other General Infromation

There is a collection of guidelines that BPA Chapters have to follow when participating in BPA events. We have these guidelines, taken directly from the BPA Chapter Handbook, layed out here as a reminder and convienent recorce.

Dress code

All compeditive events require the Professional Dress Code, outlined below, for both BPA Chapters and competing students.

Offical Officer Dress Attire:

  1. Navy blue blazer with offical BPA logo placed on left chest pocket
  2. Only the membership pin should be worn on the left lapel. No other pins or accessories should be worn on the left lapel.
  3. The following items can be worn on the right lapel of the blazer:
    1. Highest officer pin
    2. Highest earned torch pin
    3. One merit scholar pin
    4. Name badge

NLC Dress Code:

  1. Dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie
  2. Pant or skirt suit
  3. Suit. dress skirt, and tie
  4. Dress skirt or dress slacks with coordinated blouse and/or sweater
  5. Sport coat, coordinated dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie
  6. BPA blazer with dress skirt or dress slacks with blouse or sweater
  7. BPA blazer with dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie
  8. Dress shoes with socks
  9. Dress heels or closed-toed flats

*All denim is not permitted for any of the items listed above.
*Skirt length may be no greater than two inches above the top of the knee.
*No tennis shoes, sandals, flip-flops or open-toed flats
